Beyond Sunlight: Strategic Solar Site Optimisation ☀️🏜️

Project Overview

This project serves as a Strategic Investment Roadmap for the expansion of the Mohammed bin Rashid (MBR) Solar Park (Phase 7). While traditional engineering models prioritize solar irradiance, this framework introduces a Multi-Criteria Decision Analysis (MCDA) that balances energy yield against "hidden" variables: atmospheric dust loading, Northwest Shamal wind corridors, and grid interconnection CAPEX.

Key Strategic Business Outcomes:

Value Gap Identification: Identified an AED 233 Million Lifecycle Value surplus by selecting optimised Site 3 over logistically non-viable alternatives.

Risk Mitigation: Quantified a 98% correlation (R2=0.99) between geospatial risk indices and empirical energy degradation (soiling).

National Alignment: Directly supports the UAE Net Zero 2050 Strategy and Water Security Strategy 2036 by reducing projected cleaning water consumption by 15%.
